We are what we do

description

Green Steps was established in 2023 by a group of environmental enthusiasts who wanted to make a difference. Recognizing the urgent need for sustainable living practices, they dedicated their efforts to creating a platform that could educate, inspire, and empower individuals to live more sustainably.

What started as a small project has grown into a comprehensive platform that provides practical advice and tips on energy conservation, waste management, water resources management, and more. We also host a vibrant community forum where users can share their experiences, views, and ideas about sustainability.

We are committed to promoting sustainable living and fostering a community of like-minded individuals. We believe that by working together, we can make a significant impact on our environment and create a better future for all.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of individuals adopt more sustainable lifestyles and have fostered a community that is actively working towards a more sustainable future. We are proud of what we have achieved, but we know there is still much work to do.

If you would like to know more about what we do or how you can live more sustainably, please don't hesitate to reach out. You can also join our community forum to share your experiences and learn from others. We look forward to hearing from you!

Location

Our facility is located around a mile between Llawyrglyn and Trefeglwys in the beautiful county of Powys. Keep an eye open for signs for Green Steps at Carno onwards!

Directions

By car:

Follow the A470 towards Brecon, then take the B52 towards Cerist. Continue straight to Trefeglyws and take the first right by the Post Office, you can't miss us!

By public transport:

The nearest bus link is in Trefeglwys. Unfortunately, there is no longer a rail link to the nearest town.
